Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: idk
Ingredients Quantity
flour 350 g
cocoa powder 100 g
butter 250 g
sugar 210 g
vanilia suger 10 g
eggs 2 piecrs
milk 130 g
espresso coffee 130 g
lemon juice 10 g
baking powder 8 g
soda 4 g

Preparation:

Step 1: Combine the sifted flour with cocoa powder, baking powder, soda, salt.

Step 2: Mix milk with lemon juice.

Step 3: Combine soft butter with sugar, vanilla sugar and beat for 2 minutes.

Step 4: Add eggs at room temperature and continue whipping the mixture for 3-4 minutes.

Step 5: Add strong warm coffee to the milk-lemon mixture.

Step 6: To the butter-egg mixture, alternating 3 doses, add flour and liquid mixture.

Step 7: Put 150 g of dough in a pastry bag (for cookies).

Step 8: Divide the remaining dough into 4 parts of 280 g each and distribute it in a form lined

Step 9: In a well-preheated 180 C oven, bake the cakes for 20-25 minutes at an average level.

Step 10: Cool the cakes in the mold for 10 minutes, then carefully remove and cool completely on the grill.

Step 11: Draw 12 circles on baking paper using a template and form blanks from the 150 g of dough laid aside.

Step 12: Bake in a preheated 180 C oven for 10-12 minutes and cool completely.

Step 13: Trim the three cooled cakes (cut off the tops)

Cream:

Step 1: Combine the cooled sour cream with sugar, vanilla sugar and beat.

Step 2: Put the cream in a pastry bag or bag.

Assembling:

Step 1: 3 cakes, using a pastry bag, layer with cream.

Step 2: Pour the top (uncut) cake with warm fudge.

Step 3: Decorate the cake with cookies (gluing it in pairs with cream remnants), crumbs and sprinkles.

Step 4: Put the cake in the refrigerator for 5-6 hours.
